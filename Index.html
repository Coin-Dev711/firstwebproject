The Legend of Zelda

Created by Shigeru Miyamoto and Takashi Tezuka



The Legend of Zelda: Breath of the Wild is a 2017 action-adventure game developed by Nintendo for the Wii U and Nintendo Switch. It s set presumably set near the end of the Zelda timeline, now let's talk about it's development. The Legend of Zelda: Breath of the Wild was in development for around 5 years, with development in 2012 and ending in 2017, it was planned to be a launch title for the Wii U, but due to the game's scope and ambition, it was delayed multiple times. The game was eventually released as a launch title for the Nintendo Switch, missing the deadline by a long margin in March 2017, alongside the Wii U version. The game received near instant critical acclaim upon release, with many praising its design, attention to detail, and successful innovative gameplay mechanics. It won multiple awards, including Game of the Year awards from various gaming publications and award shows. The game has sold over 30 million copies worldwide across both platforms, making it one of the best-selling games of all time.



Final Fantasy VII

Created by Hironobu Sakaguchi



Final Fantasy VII is a pretty old game (you can tell from the low quality image) but is still respected and talked about, even today, it's a 1997 role-playing video game developed and published by Square (now Square Enix) for the PlayStation. It's the seventh main installment in the Final Fantasy series and was directed by Yoshinori Kitase and produced by Hironobu Sakaguchi. Since it's was made in the 90s, they were just transitioning from 2D to 3D graphics, so the game features 3D character models and pre-rendered backgrounds, not to mention, they moved from developing for Nintendo to Sony's PlayStation, which was a big deal back then. not to mentioned, even with all that, they hade a huge plan for the game which required high bills and long hours put in by every developer who contributed to the project and finally, the game was planned to be released internationally, so it still had to be translated and this was before Google Translate. Although, evene with all these factors stacked against them, they still managed to create an excellent game with a striving community and continue their series.
